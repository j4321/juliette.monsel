Bicycle mechanics instructor

2020 – current:

Cykelköket (Gothenburg, Sweden)

2018 – 2020:

uN p’Tit véLo dAnS La Tête (Grenoble, France)

I have been volunteering since 2017 in DIY bicycle workshops that aim at promoting cycling, learning mechanics and reusing of unused bicycles. First, I learned bicycle mechanics by going every Wednesday at the “volunteer evening” at uN p’Tit véLo dAnS La Tête, where we took apart and repaired bikes for the workshop. Then, I also came at normal opening hours to share my knowledge and explain to people how to fix their bikes. I have been doing the same at Cykelköket Göteborg since I moved to Gothenburg.
